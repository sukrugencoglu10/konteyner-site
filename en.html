About Us

Founded in 2018, PEKCON CONTAINER operates in container supply chain management and international transportation. Based in Istanbul, our company serves with a wide agency network. With our worldwide agency network, our company continues to provide services in FCL full container, LCL partial container, domestic transportation, ship charter, transit trade transportation, and combined transportation to many regions in Africa, Europe, and America, especially in the Far East, Middle East, and Mediterranean Countries, with quality, reliable, professional service understanding and wide agency connections.

With an unconditional customer satisfaction approach, our company sees our valued customers as business and logistics solution partners; in a position that produces solutions, not problems; never compromises ethical rules; with boutique approaches, maximum care at every stage of the transportation process and accurate, up-to-date information transfer, our company has chosen long-term relationships as a goal.
